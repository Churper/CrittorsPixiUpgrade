Privacy Policy

Crittors is a browser-based game. Your privacy matters to us.

Data We Store
Game progress, settings, and currency are saved locally in your browser (localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to submit a leaderboard score.

Advertising
We use Google AdSense and AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. Google may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads. See Google's ad policy for details.

Cookies
We do not set cookies ourselves. Third-party ad providers may set cookies as described above.

Personal Information
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Leaderboard names are optional and stored on our server with no other identifying data.

Children
This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes
This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes take effect immediately upon being posted in-game.
